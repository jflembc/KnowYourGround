Data Protection Policy
Effective April 1, 2025 · Know Your Ground
Your farm's financial data is sensitive business information. This document explains exactly how we protect it — technically, operationally, and contractually.

1. Storage & Encryption

All data is stored in a Supabase PostgreSQL database hosted in the United States. Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256. Data in transit uses TLS 1.2 or higher. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt and never stored in plaintext.

Authentication uses JSON Web Tokens (JWTs) with automatic expiry and silent refresh to maintain security during active sessions without disrupting your workflow.

2. Access Controls

Your data is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) policies enforced at the database level. This means one user physically cannot query another user's farm data — it is blocked at the database query layer, not just the application layer.

  • All tables enforce user_id = auth.uid() policies
  • No admin access to your data without your explicit consent
  • Internal access to production data is logged and audited

3. Backups

Supabase performs automated daily backups with point-in-time recovery available for the previous 7 days. Backups are encrypted at rest and stored in geographically separate facilities.

4. Breach Notification

In the event of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you by email within 72 hours of confirmation, including the nature of the breach, data affected, steps taken, and what you can do to protect yourself.

5. Data Portability

You can export all farm and field financial data at any time via Summary → Export for Bank (CSV format, compatible with Excel and most accounting software). You own your data and can take it with you.

6. Account Deletion

  • Immediately: Account deactivated, login disabled
  • Within 24 hours: Active data purged from live database
  • Within 30 days: Data removed from all backup snapshots
  • Billing records: Retained 7 years per financial regulations

Export before you delete. Data recovery is not possible after deletion. Always export your data first.

7. Sub-Processors

  • Supabase, Inc. — Database, auth, file storage. SOC 2 Type II certified.
  • Stripe, Inc. — Payment processing. PCI DSS Level 1 certified.
  • Cloudflare, Inc. — CDN and DDoS protection.

Each processor is bound by data processing agreements requiring protection of your data in accordance with applicable law.

Data Protection Contact

Data deletion, export requests, or security concerns: data@knowyourground.com

Know Your Ground is a farm financial planning tool. The numbers it produces are estimates based on data you enter. They are not a substitute for professional financial, tax, or agronomic advice.

READ THIS. Before making major decisions based on numbers from this platform, consult a qualified farm financial advisor, CPA, or lender. Farm financial decisions can have significant and long-lasting consequences.

1. No Professional Advice

Know Your Ground is software. It is not a licensed financial advisor, accountant, tax professional, agronomist, or commodity broker. Nothing produced by this Platform constitutes professional advice of any kind.

2. Estimates Are Based on Your Inputs

All calculations depend entirely on the accuracy of data you enter. We make no warranties that projections will match actual farm income, costs, or yields. Factors outside the Platform's scope include:

  • Weather events, drought, hail, flood, or crop disease
  • Commodity price volatility and basis fluctuation
  • Changes in government farm program payments (ARC, PLC)
  • Input cost inflation (fuel, fertilizer, chemicals, seed)
  • Equipment failure, unplanned repairs, or labor shortages
  • Changes in lease terms, land values, or financing rates

3. Market Prices Are Not Real-Time

Any market or elevator price displayed in the Platform is either manually entered by you or provided as a reference. It does not constitute a live market quote, a bid from any elevator, or a contractual offer. Always confirm current prices directly with your elevator or broker before contracting.

4. Tax Implications

The Platform does not calculate, account for, or advise on tax liabilities, depreciation schedules, or tax treatment of any farm income or expense. Consult a qualified CPA for all tax decisions.

5. Limitation of Liability

Know Your Ground shall not be liable for any financial loss, crop loss, contracting loss, tax liability, or any other damages arising from reliance on any data, calculation, projection, or summary produced by the Platform. You use this tool at your own judgment and risk.

The CSV export is provided as a convenience for preparing lender documentation. Lenders should independently verify all figures. We make no representations regarding the acceptability of Platform-generated reports for any financing purpose.

We want you to feel confident before you pay a single dollar. Here is exactly how billing works for every plan — monthly, annual, and lifetime — so there are no surprises.

Know Your Ground offers three pricing options:

  • Monthly — $100/month: Billed each month. Cancel anytime. Access ends at the close of the paid period.
  • Annual — $1,000/year: Billed once per year. Cancel anytime. Access continues through the end of the paid year.

All prices are in USD. Prices are subject to change for new subscribers with advance notice. Existing subscribers on annual or lifetime plans are not affected by price increases during their paid period or lifetime, respectively.

2. Cancellation — Monthly & Annual

Cancel anytime from your account settings. Cancellation immediately stops future billing. Your access continues until the end of the period you have already paid for — we do not cut access early.

Example: You pay the annual plan on January 1st and cancel on March 1st. You keep full access through December 31st. No partial refund is issued for unused months by default.

3. Lifetime Plan — Terms

The Lifetime plan grants permanent access to Know Your Ground for as long as the product is commercially operated. Key terms:

  • Payment is one-time and non-recurring
  • Access includes all standard features available to annual subscribers at the time of purchase, and all future standard feature updates
  • If the product is discontinued, lifetime subscribers will receive at least 12 months advance notice and a prorated refund calculated from the date of discontinuation notice
  • Lifetime accounts are non-transferable and tied to the individual who purchased them
  • Lifetime pricing is introductory and subject to increase — your locked-in price is honored permanently once purchased

Note on product continuity: Know Your Ground is a privately operated product. While we intend to operate indefinitely, no guarantee of perpetual operation can be made. The 12-month notice and prorated refund provision is our commitment to treating lifetime subscribers fairly in any discontinuation scenario.

4. When We Issue Refunds

All plan fees are generally non-refundable. We make exceptions in these circumstances:

  • 14-day new subscriber guarantee: Any first-time subscriber — on any plan including Lifetime — may request a full refund within 14 days of their initial payment, no questions asked.
  • Billing errors: Incorrect charges, double charges, or charges after cancellation are refunded immediately upon confirmation.
  • Extended platform outage: If the Platform is unavailable for more than 72 continuous hours, affected subscribers may request a pro-rated credit for the verified downtime period.
  • Exceptional circumstances: We review requests on a case-by-case basis. We are a small, farmer-focused business and we want to be fair. Reach out before assuming the answer is no.

5. How to Request a Refund

Email billing@knowyourground.com with your account email, the amount charged, your plan type, and your reason. We respond within 3 business days. Approved refunds are returned to your original payment method within 5–10 business days.

6. Chargebacks

Initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer without first contacting us will result in immediate account suspension pending investigation. We will provide full transaction documentation to your card issuer. Contact us first — disputes are almost always resolved faster and more fairly by talking directly.

Yes. You can upgrade at any time. When upgrading from monthly to annual, we prorate your remaining monthly days toward the annual price. When upgrading to lifetime, your remaining subscription period is credited toward the $4,500 payment.
What happens to my data if I cancel?
Your data stays in your account for 90 days after cancellation so you can reactivate or export it. After 90 days it is permanently deleted. You can export everything to CSV at any time before then. Lifetime accounts never expire.
Is the lifetime plan really permanent?
Yes — for the life of the product. If we ever discontinue Know Your Ground, lifetime subscribers receive at least 12 months advance notice and a prorated refund. Full terms are in the Refund Policy.
Will the lifetime price go up?
The current $4,500 lifetime price is introductory. As the product matures and we add more features, the lifetime price will increase. Once you purchase, your price is locked permanently — you will never be charged again.
Is there a free trial?
We offer a 14-day full refund guarantee instead of a free trial. Sign up, use the full product for up to 14 days, and if it isn't for you, email us for a complete refund — no questions asked.
Do you offer discounts for farm co-ops or multiple operations?
We are open to discussing group or co-op arrangements. Email billing@knowyourground.com to talk through what makes sense for your situation.
