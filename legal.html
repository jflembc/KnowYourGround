Data Protection
Effective April 1, 2025 · Know Your Ground
Your farm financial data is sensitive business information. Here is exactly how we protect it.

1. Storage & Encryption

Supabase PostgreSQL hosted in the US. Data at rest: AES-256. Data in transit: TLS 1.2+. Passwords: bcrypt hash, never plaintext. JWTs expire automatically with silent refresh.

2. Access Controls

Row Level Security (RLS) enforced at database level. One user physically cannot query another user's farm data.

  • All tables enforce user_id = auth.uid()
  • No admin access without explicit consent
  • Internal production access logged and audited

3. Backups

Automated daily backups via Supabase. 7-day point-in-time recovery. Encrypted at rest. Geographically separate storage.

4. Breach Notification

Email notification within 72 hours of confirmed breach — including nature, data affected, steps taken, and what you can do.

5. Portability

Export all data anytime via Summary → Export for Bank (CSV format). You own your data and can take it with you.

6. Deletion Timeline

  • Immediately: Account deactivated
  • Within 24 hours: Active data purged
  • Within 30 days: Removed from all backups
  • Billing records: Retained 7 years

Export before you delete. Recovery is not possible after deletion.

7. Sub-Processors

  • Supabase, Inc. — SOC 2 Type II certified
  • Stripe, Inc. — PCI DSS Level 1 certified
  • Cloudflare, Inc. — CDN and DDoS protection
